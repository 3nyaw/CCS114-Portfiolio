GOOD STUDY HABITS TO DEVELOP

Anybody can develop good study habits to make studying more effective, efficient, and enjoyable

  1. Find a good place to study.
  2. Minimize distractions.
    • Turn-off your wifi
    • Be mindful of your phone.
  3. Take breaks.
    • Take a short walk
    • Listen to a mood-boosting song
    • Stretch
    • Meditate
    • Have a snack
    • Take a shower
  4. Set study goals for each session. Take practice tests.
    • Create flashcards
    • Write your own questions
    • Have a friend quiz you
  5. Use your own words.
